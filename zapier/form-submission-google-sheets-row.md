## 📝 What it does
Appends every new form response as a row in Google Sheets.

## 🎯 Use when
> When you need a simple data log of submissions.

## ⚡ Trigger
New form submission

## 🏁 Steps
1. Trigger on submission
2. Map fields to columns
3. Append row
4. Optional Slack notify

## ⚠️ Note
Keep the sheet header in sync with form fields.
