## 📝 What it does
On a new Calendly booking, creates/updates a CRM record and schedules reminders.

## 🎯 Use when
> When meetings should sync to CRM automatically.

## ⚡ Trigger
New Calendly event

## 🏁 Steps
1. Trigger on booking
2. Upsert CRM contact
3. Create calendar reminder
4. Send confirmation

## ⚠️ Note
Handle reschedules/cancellations with extra triggers.
