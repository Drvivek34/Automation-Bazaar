## 📝 What it does
Turns starred/labeled emails into tasks in your project tool.

## 🎯 Use when
> When email is your informal task inbox.

## ⚡ Trigger
New labeled email

## 🏁 Steps
1. Trigger on labeled email
2. Extract subject/body
3. Create task (Todoist/Asana)
4. Link back to email

## ⚠️ Note
Use a dedicated label to avoid task spam.
