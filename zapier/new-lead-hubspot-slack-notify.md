## 📝 What it does
Adds new leads to HubSpot and pings sales in Slack.

## 🎯 Use when
> When inbound leads must reach CRM + team fast.

## ⚡ Trigger
New lead (form/ads)

## 🏁 Steps
1. Trigger on new lead
2. Create/update HubSpot contact
3. Notify Slack
4. Optionally start a sequence

## ⚠️ Note
Map UTM fields for attribution.
