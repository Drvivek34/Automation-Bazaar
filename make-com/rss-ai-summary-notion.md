## 📝 What it does
Pulls new RSS items, summarizes with an AI module, and stores them in a Notion database.

## 🎯 Use when
> When you build a curated reading database.

## ⚡ Trigger
New RSS item

## 🏁 Steps
1. Watch RSS feed
2. Summarize with AI
3. Create Notion page
4. Tag by topic

## ⚠️ Note
Add a dedupe filter on item GUID.
