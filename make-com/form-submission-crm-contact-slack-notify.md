## 📝 What it does
Watches a form, creates/updates a CRM contact, and posts a notification to Slack.

## 🎯 Use when
> When new form leads must reach your CRM and team instantly.

## ⚡ Trigger
New form submission

## 🏁 Steps
1. Watch form (Typeform/Tally)
2. Search/create CRM contact
3. Map fields
4. Post to Slack channel

## ⚠️ Note
Use a router to branch on lead source.
