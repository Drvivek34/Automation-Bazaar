## 📝 What it does
Takes a content row and publishes/schedules it across several social platforms.

## 🎯 Use when
> When you publish the same content to many channels.

## ⚡ Trigger
New row in content sheet

## 🏁 Steps
1. Watch content sheet
2. Format per platform
3. Publish/schedule
4. Mark row as posted

## ⚠️ Note
Stagger post times to avoid duplicate-content penalties.
