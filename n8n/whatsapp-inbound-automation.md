## 📝 What it does
Handles inbound WhatsApp messages, routes by intent, and replies or creates a ticket.

## 🎯 Use when
> When you support customers over WhatsApp.

## ⚡ Trigger
WhatsApp message

## 🏁 Steps
1. Receive message
2. Detect intent with the LLM
3. Reply or open a ticket
4. Log to CRM

## ⚠️ Note
Follow WhatsApp Business policy for automated messaging.
