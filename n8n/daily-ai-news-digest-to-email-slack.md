## 📝 What it does
Aggregates RSS/news sources, summarizes with an LLM, and sends a daily digest.

## 🎯 Use when
> When you want a curated morning briefing.

## ⚡ Trigger
Schedule (daily)

## 🏁 Steps
1. Pull configured feeds
2. De-dupe + rank items
3. Summarize with the LLM
4. Send digest to email/Slack

## ⚠️ Note
Cap item count to keep the digest skimmable.
