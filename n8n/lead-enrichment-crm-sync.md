## 📝 What it does
Enriches new leads (company, role, socials) and upserts them into the CRM with a Slack alert.

## 🎯 Use when
> When inbound leads need enrichment before sales follow-up.

## ⚡ Trigger
New lead (form/webhook)

## 🏁 Steps
1. Receive lead
2. Enrich via data provider
3. Upsert to CRM (HubSpot/Pipedrive)
4. Notify sales in Slack

## ⚠️ Note
Respect data-privacy rules for enrichment providers.

lead enrichment, crm, hubspot, sales
