## 📝 What it does
Reads incoming Gmail, classifies by topic/urgency with an LLM, and drafts a context-aware reply for review.

## 🎯 Use when
> When your inbox needs triage and first-draft replies at scale.

## ⚡ Trigger
New email in Gmail

## 🏁 Steps
1. Trigger on new Gmail message
2. Classify topic + urgency with GPT-4/Claude
3. Draft a reply from a template + context
4. Save as draft or send after approval

## ⚠️ Note
Keep a human-approval step until reply quality is trusted.
